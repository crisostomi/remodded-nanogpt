The engine lives in substrate era E15 (single-saved-activation). Its 24 registered genes are the E15-era additive/allele genes; the other ~147 gene-rows are older substrates/tunings not yet renderable (see Coverage).

The four gene kinds how every change is classified

cheap · independently toggleable

additive

A slot-free feature that layers onto the current scaffold without removing anything (e.g. smear, value_embed_gates). Most registered genes are additive.

mutually-exclusive · occupies a slot

allele

One variant of a named slot (allele_group). Selecting it replaces the prior variant — e.g. orthogonalizer: Newton-Schulz ↔ Polar Express. 10 slots exist; the orthogonalizer slot is the first implemented in the engine.

expensive · partitions history into eras

substrate

A structural rewrite of the model/optimizer/forward scaffold that all later genes attach to. Each opens an era (E0…E15).

no new mechanism · config dimension

tuning

Pure hyperparameter / schedule / layout changes (LR, betas, iterations, version bumps). In this engine these are searchable optim.* overrides + the TRAINING_STAGES curriculum, not genes.

Registered gene library 24 genes · src/nano/features/

Every gene declares the surfaces it touches via a FeatureSpec: owned params/buffers, requires / conflicts / soft_conflicts, and modifies_* flags. Toggleable genes are fully guarded across construction, the optimizer param table and the hot forward; structural genes are always-on for now (the builder refuses to disable them).

Allele slots 10 mutually-exclusive variant slots · GENE_MAP §3

Each slot holds exactly one active variant at any record. The variant advances over time; selecting a new one replaces the old. Green marks the variant active in the current record. The allele mechanism (FeatureSpec.allele_group, exactly-one-of) is now implemented; the orthogonalizer slot is the first switchable one in the engine (polar_express ↔ newton_schulz). The other slots are tracked but not yet switchable (HANDOFF §7b).
